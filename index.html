You Will Not Survive is an Action Roguelike that combines RPG Leveling, Dungeon Crawling, and Fast-Paced Bullet Hell Combat. Embrace death in this dark fantasy adventure as you try to discover your purpose and origins within The Forsaken Lands. Only you can decide the fate of this scarred world.
